## Reel Structure (under 60 seconds)

```
0:00–0:03  Hook (one sentence, on screen as text + spoken)
0:03–0:08  Context (why this matters — 1 sentence)
0:08–0:45  Show it — screen recording, result, before/after (not just talking)
0:45–0:55  What I learned / what's next
0:55–1:00  CTA: "Follow for week [N+1]" or "Link in bio for full plan"
```

**Algorithm rule in 2026:** Raw, authentic, human proof. Instagram actively suppresses templated/generic AI-generated content. Must show yourself, your screen, your real results.

## Viral Hook Templates (15 tested formats)

**Journey/accountability (primary format for Months 1–2):**
1. "Day 1 of learning AI from zero. No coding experience. Here's the plan."
2. "I've never written a line of code. I'm building an AI app in 6 months. Week [N] update."
3. "Most people spend 2 years learning AI. I'm trying to do it in 6 months."
4. "I published my first website today. I had no idea how to do this 30 days ago."

**Revelation (Month 3+ when you have technical content):**
5. "I trained my first AI model. It was way simpler than I thought."
6. "You don't need a CS degree to get an AI job. Here's what you actually need."
7. "I asked Claude to explain machine learning like I'm 5. This actually worked."
8. "The AI engineering job market in 2026 is weird. Here's what I found."

**Failure/honesty (high engagement, builds trust):**
9. "I tried to build an AI app this week. It completely broke."
10. "Week [N] was rough. Here's what I learned from failing."
11. "I almost quit learning to code this week. This is why I didn't."

**Contrast/before-after:**
12. "30 days ago: [blank screen]. Today: [dashboard live]." (side by side)
13. "What my code looked like week 1 vs. week 8."
14. "I went from knowing nothing about AI to [specific milestone]. Here's the actual path."

**Educational 1-tip (Month 3+):**
15. "The one Python concept that unlocked everything else for me."

**Hook formula:** Contradiction OR specific number OR "I did X so you don't have to" + visual proof in frame. Never start with "Hey guys" or "Today I'm going to..."

## Posting Tactics

- **Time:** 8–10am or 8–11pm local time
- **Audio:** Use trending audio from Reels tab — check trending tab before filming each week
- **Engagement window:** After posting, reply to every comment within 30 minutes for the first hour. Signals algorithm.
- **Saves:** Add a text card at end of every Reel: "Save this to remember it." Saves are the highest-value engagement signal.
- **Static posts:** Carousel format, 5–7 slides. Slide 1 = hook question. Slides 2–6 = one answer each. Slide 7 = "Save + follow."

## Weekly Research System (20 min every Sunday)

1. Find 5–10 AI learning accounts with 2K–50K followers. Sort their Reels by views. Study the top 3 per account: what was the hook? what format? what topic?
2. Check Instagram's Reels tab for trending audio. Save 3 to use next week.
3. Review Meta Business Suite analytics: reach per hashtag, watch-through rate per Reel.
4. Save Reels you naturally stopped scrolling at during the week. At end of week, ask why you stopped.
5. Log hook patterns in `research.md` in the repo.

**Hashtags to search for competitor accounts:** #ailearning, #learningpython, #techjourneybeginners, #codingjourney, #aiengineering

## Collab Post Strategy

From Month 2 onward: 2 collab posts per month. Target accounts with 2K–15K followers in AI learning / tech education niche. DM offer: "Hey, I'm building in public on my AI learning journey — would you be open to a collab post?" One DM per week from Month 2 Week 3.

Collab Posts (Meta's feature) share the post to both audiences simultaneously. Highest organic reach multiplier available to small accounts.

## Monetization Roadmap

### Phase 1: 0–2K followers (Months 1–3) — Build the asset
- No selling yet. Build email list via free downloads.
- **Free download to create in Month 2:** `learning-plan-template.md` OR PDF of the 6-month plan. Host on GitHub or Gumroad free tier. Collect emails via Google Forms.
- Email list converts 10–40x better than Instagram for selling.

### Phase 2: 2K–5K followers (Months 3–5) — Affiliate income
Realistic: $50–200/month

| Program | What to promote |
|---------|----------------|
| Brilliant.org | Math/CS courses (~25% commission) |
| DataCamp | Data science courses (~30–40%) |
| CapCut | Video editing tool |
| Hostinger / GitHub Pro | Hosting/dev tools |
| Anthropic/OpenAI referral | API credits (check current programs) |

Promote naturally: "tools I actually use to learn AI" content.

### Phase 3: 5K–10K followers (Months 4–6) — First paid product
Pick one:

**A) "AI Learning OS" template pack ($15–25 on Gumroad)**
- The `progress.json` template + dashboard HTML + setup guide
- Reel: "I spent 2 months building this. You can clone it in 30 minutes."

**B) 1-hour coaching call ($50–75)**
- "I'll review your AI learning plan and tell you what to build first"
- Book via Calendly, pay via Stripe/PayPal
- 1 call/week = $200–300/month

**C) Brand deal ($100–300/post at 3K+ engaged followers)**
- AI tools, coding courses, productivity apps want micro-influencers
- 73% of brands prefer under-50K accounts for higher engagement
- Reach out proactively; don't wait to be found

### Phase 4: 10K+ followers — Full stack
- **Instagram Subscription** ($3–9/month, exclusive content)
- **Course on Gumroad/Teachable:** "Zero to AI Engineer in 6 Months" ($49–97)
- **Cohort program:** 10 people × $150–300 = $1,500–3,000/cohort

Realistic 10K milestone revenue (multiple streams): $500–1,500/month
